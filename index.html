Animal group names

For many species of animals, there are names that we use to refer to a group of them - like a flock of birds. Here are a few you might not know.

  • A skulk of foxes.
    Also called a "leash."
  • A clowder of cats.
    Also called a "pounce", "glaring", "kindle", or "clutter."
